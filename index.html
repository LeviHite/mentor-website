Getting Started

Up and running
in minutes.

No IT department required. Mentor is built so ministry leaders — not developers — can get their community going fast.

01
Create your ministry account
Sign up at app.mentordiscipleship.com, choose your plan, and set up your organization profile. Takes under five minutes — no credit card required to start.
02
Invite your people
Add mentors, mentees, group leaders, and members by email or invite link. Assign roles, create groups, and structure your ministry the way it actually works.
03
Walk together in faith
Track relationships, run Bible studies, post prayer requests, and watch your community grow — all from one unified platform built for discipleship.
